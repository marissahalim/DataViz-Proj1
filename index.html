About the Cholera Dashboard

Project 1 Cholera is produced by Marissa Halim using the Plot.ly Javascript Library. The data comes from Robin Wilson who is a Freelancer in Remote Sensing, GIS, Data Science and Python. The information of the deaths in the UK based on age and sex is from this site . The information of the deaths in Naples is from page 119 in the book Naples in the Time of Cholera 1884-1911.
